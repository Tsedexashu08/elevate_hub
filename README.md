## 📱 Overview

**Elevate Hub** is a mobile-first platform revolutionizing how Africans discover, join, and manage events across the continent. From national sports tournaments and tech hackathons to professional networking mixers, major concerts, cultural festivals, and high-level diplomatic gatherings (UN, AU, NGOs, government summits) — we bring every gathering that matters into one seamless experience.

## ✨ Key Features

### For Event Goers
- 🔍 **Smart Discovery** - Browse and filter events by category, location, date, price, organizer type, and keywords
- 🎫 **Seamless Registration** - One-tap RSVP, secure ticket purchasing with integrated payment gateways
- 🎯 **Personalized Recommendations** - AI-powered suggestions based on interests, past attendance, and location
- 📍 **Event Companion** - Access agendas, live updates, maps, speaker lists, sponsors, and live-stream links
- 💬 **Networking Tools** - In-app chat groups, business card exchange, and post-event follow-up tools
- ⏰ **Smart Reminders** - Save to calendar, get timely notifications, and share invites easily

### For Organizers
- 📊 **Powerful Dashboard** - Create professional event pages with ease
- 💳 **Ticket Management** - Sell tickets, manage registrations, and track attendance in real-time
- 📈 **Analytics & Insights** - Collect feedback and access detailed event performance metrics
- 👥 **Attendee Management** - Engage with participants before, during, and after events
